What is Universal Title Controller?

Universal Title Controller is application for controlling vMix through official HTTP API, using vMix Shortcuts.

Create your own layout for controlling lower-thirds, scorebugs and other titles, switch inputs, automate repeating actions.

In some cases UTC can be your third hand.

A bunch of widgets to control vMix
Universal Title Controller Features

Realtime Title Controller

Link title text into vMix with text field into UTC. See that editing text into UTC changes text into vMix.

Scripting Even On Non-Pro vMix Editions

Automate your controller with scripts, that support expressions, conditions and jumps.

 

Hotkeys

Assign script or event to key or combination of keys onto your keyboard.

Event Scheduling

Start any of your scripts at specific day and time.

Audio Controlling

Make your own audio mixer for control and view vMix busses and inputs audio.

Layout Organization

With colored captions, info regions and labels.

External Data Plugins

Load data from XML or local Excel files, look at vMix outputs with NDI monitor plugin.
